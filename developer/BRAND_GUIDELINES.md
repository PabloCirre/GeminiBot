# 🤖 PIBOT: Official Brand Guidelines

PIBOT is a professional, high-performance tool for autonomous AI orchestration. Our brand identity reflects **Precision, Sovereignty, and Modernity**.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette
The brand moves away from generic greens and blues toward a high-contrast, vibrant cyberpunk aesthetic.

| Color | Hex | Role | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vibrant Cyan** | `#3FFDFA` | Primary | Main accents, focus rings, primary bot icon. |
| **Intense Pink** | `#FC5FC2` | Secondary | Hover states, specific highlights, depth glows. |
| **Deep Space** | `#0A0A0C` | Background | Primary window background. |
| **Glass White** | `rgba(255,255,255,0.05)` | Surface | Cards, panels, and translucent layers. |

---

## 🖼️ Iconography: "The Minimal Bot"
- **Shape:** Minimalist robot head with circular eyes (Source: SVGRepo 521818).
- **Style:** Flat design. No gradients, no text, no complex shadows. 
- **Application:** Use as a solid Cyan `#3FFDFA` SVG for the macOS Dock and System Tray.

---

## 🔡 Typography: "Inter" & "JetBrains Mono"
- **Interface:** **Inter** (Variable). Clean, geometric, and professional.
- **Code & Shell:** **JetBrains Mono** or **Roboto Mono**. Optimized for readability during long terminal sessions.

---

## 🎭 Design Philosophy
1.  **Zero-Clutter:** Every element must serve a functional agents-driven purpose.
2.  **Translucency:** Use Glassmorphism (blur + low opacity) to maintain a feeling of depth and modernity.
3.  **Proactive Contrast:** High-saturation colors should only be used to guide the user's eye toward actions (Proposed Commands, Status changes).
